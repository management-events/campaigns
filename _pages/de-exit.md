			<h2>Concept</h2>		
		The 9th annual 600Minutes Executive IT in Germany continues the path of exploring the latest and most provocative digital topics. This year’s edition takes the IT landscape beyond usual topics. Focusing on the impact, challenges, and opportunities within Artificial Intelligence and other emerging technologies in future business developments. With a history of 13 years in 11 countries in Europe and Asia, 600Minutes Executive IT is a unique invitation-only platform for peer-to-peer networking. Join more than 200 CIOs and other IT decision makers to gain foresight on the latest technology developments
